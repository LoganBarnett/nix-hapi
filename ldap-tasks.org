* TODO Group multi-member reconciliation is broken              :bug:ldap:
  :PROPERTIES:
  :ID:       L1
  :END:

  ~reconcile.rs:133-136~: ~mock_resolved~ joins multi-valued attributes with
  ~"\n"~ into a single ~ResolvedFieldValue::Managed~.  ~diff_attrs~ then
